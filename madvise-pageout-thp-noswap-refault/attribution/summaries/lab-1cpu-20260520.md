the no-swap reclaim split/failure path.

## Interpretation

The lab attribution supports a narrower conclusion:

```text
With default/hugepage requests, v6.19.9 is actually THP-backed and repeatedly
enters split_folio_to_list() in the no-swap MADV_PAGEOUT reclaim path. v6.12.77
is not actually THP-backed and does not enter split_folio_to_list(). In the
nohugepage same-state control, the old-faster signal disappears.
```

The original upstream report should therefore be corrected rather than pushed
as a proven same-state regression. A follow-up should explain the no-swap
pageout/refault wording issue, clarify the `cycle_ns_per_page` metric, report
the actual THP backing difference, and ask whether a no-swap THP split
fast-fail/skip-split optimization discussion is still useful.
